ye hai p tag jo ki paragraph likhne ke kaam aati hai. wahi agar p tag ke andar jo bhi likha h uski styling karni hai aur uski alag css file nahi banani to mai p ke andar hi styling kar sakte hu, usse inline css bhi kehte hai. jo bhi mai p tag ke saath likhunga wo attribute ho jayega p tag ka. Wo attribute sirf p tag me hi kaam karega. This goes with every other tag. jaise iss p tag me jo styling ka attribute diya hai uss karan hi iss paragraph ke peeche black background hai aur text white hai.
