Video Tag

As the name suggests videos tag videos ko apni website pe play karne ke liye use kiya jaata hai. iske apne bhi kuch attributes hoote hai jaiseki -
src -
isme aap apne video source ka link daalenge jooki aapke computer ya internet pe bhi ho sakta hai.
control -
isme aap apne video ke controls like play pause mute and download laa sakte hai.
muted -
isse aap apne video ko mute rakh sakte hai baadme user apne aap unmute karke dekh sakte hai.
autoplay -
isse video apne aap website load hote hi play hota hai.
width/height -
isse aap video ki height/width set kar sakte hai.
poster -
isse aap apne video pe thumbnail daal sakte hai.

Audio Tag

As the name suggests audio tag audios ko apni website pe play karne ke liye use kiya jaata hai. iske apne bhi kuch attributes hoote hai jaiseki -
src -
isme aap apne audio source ka link daalenge jooki aapke computer ya internet pe bhi ho sakta hai.
control -
isme aap apne video ke controls like play pause mute and download laa sakte hai.
muted -
isse aap apne video ko mute rakh sakte hai baadme user apne aap unmute karke dekh sakte hai.
autoplay -
isse video apne aap website load hote hi play hota hai.
preload -
ye specify karta hai ki audio kaise load hoga jab website load hoga. isme 3 values hoti hai - auto(decided by the browser), metadata(loads only metadata of the audio), none(dont load while loading website)
