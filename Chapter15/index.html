CSS box model is a model that helps developers and designers to make their website according to that model. the model comprises of content space, padding, border and margin. The inner most part of model is content space where all the webpage content will be present, just after that comes padding which is spacing between the content and the space provided for the content. Next comes border of the content which is done over the padding and content area. Lastly, comes the margin which covers the overall webpage and decides the spacing from all sides of the page.
